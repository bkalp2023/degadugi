Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 29, 2024

Welcome to DegaDugi. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. No Data Collection

DegaDugi is designed with your privacy as a top priority. Our application facilitates direct, peer-to-peer (P2P) connections for file and message transfers exclusively within your local network. We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your personal data, files, messages, or IP addresses.

2. Local Network Operation

All communication and data transfer initiated through our application occurs directly between your devices on your local Wi-Fi or wired network. Your data never passes through or is stored on any of our servers or any third-party servers. The connection is entirely self-contained within your local environment.

3. How It Works

Our website uses a WebSocket service solely to generate a unique, temporary ID for the QR code. This ID helps your devices discover each other on the local network to establish a direct connection. This service does not log or store any information related to your session. Once the connection is made, all subsequent communication is direct and does not involve our servers.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
